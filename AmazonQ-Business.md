# AmazonQ-Business

- Fully Managed Gen-AI assistant for your employees
- Based on your company knowledge and data.
  - Answer questions,provide summeries generate content,automate tasks
  - Perform routine actions
- Built on Amazon Bedrock(But you cant choose the underlying FMs)

- Users can be authenticated throught IAM identity Center
- Users receive response generated only from the documents they have access to
- Other external Idp can be configured(Google,AD)
- Block certian topics like Guard rails, Block keywords
- Respond only with internal information
