# Transfer Learning
the broader concept of re-using a pre-trained model to adapt it to a new related task.

Train a pre-trained model further on your data
Example: Train a model on company tax documents

Note:  Fine Tuning is specfic kind of transfer learning

Usecases:-
- A chatbot designed with particular persona/tone.
- Training using more up to date information than what the language model previously accessed
- Training with exclusive data(emails, messages records and)
